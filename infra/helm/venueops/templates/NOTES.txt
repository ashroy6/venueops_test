VenueOps Helm chart deployed.

Local render command:

  helm template venueops infra/helm/venueops --values infra/helm/venueops/values-dev.yaml

Production deployment example:

  helm upgrade --install venueops infra/helm/venueops \
    --namespace venueops \
    --create-namespace \
    --values infra/helm/venueops/values-prod.yaml \
    --set global.imageRegistry=<acr-name>.azurecr.io \
    --set global.imageTag=<git-sha> \
    --set global.workloadIdentity.clientId=<managed-identity-client-id>
